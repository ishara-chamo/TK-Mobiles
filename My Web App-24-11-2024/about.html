About TK Mobiles

Your trusted partner for all mobile device repairs since 2010. We combine technical expertise with exceptional customer service.

Our Story

Founded in 2010 by a team of passionate technicians, TK Mobiles started as a small repair shop in Colombo. Today, we've grown into one of the most trusted mobile repair services in Sri Lanka, with thousands of satisfied customers and a reputation for quality repairs at affordable prices.

What sets us apart is our commitment to using only high-quality parts and our team of certified technicians who undergo regular training to stay updated with the latest repair techniques and technologies.

Our Mission

To provide fast, reliable, and affordable repair services while maintaining the highest standards of quality and customer service. We believe in fixing devices right the first time, using only the best parts available.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted mobile repair service in Sri Lanka by continuing to innovate our repair processes, expanding our services, and maintaining our commitment to customer satisfaction.
